Ballroom dance

The sport that I have historically enjoyed the most is Ballroom dance. I first found it in middle school, when one of my best friend's older sisters invited me to attend the school ballroom class with her, seeing as they needed more guys. I quickly fell in love with this art, and then competed it for almost 6 years.

There are 2 main styles of Ballroom Dance; here they are, with their respective dances.

  1. Standard
    • Waltz
    • Viennese Waltz
    • Foxtrot
    • Quickstep
    • Tango
  2. Latin
    • Samba
    • Rhumba
    • Cha-Cha
    • Jive
    • Pasodoble
